## The moat has shifted from what you build to how you treat people.

In the age of AI, technical capability is increasingly commoditized. What remains defensible? **Kindness.**

This is where we explore that thesis. Not as theory. Not as consulting advice. But grounded in how it actually shows up in organizations navigating change, in leaders building for the future, and in individuals trying to figure out how to lead when everything is shifting.
